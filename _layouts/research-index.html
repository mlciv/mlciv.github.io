I am a post-doctoral researcher at NSF AI Institute for Student-AI Teaming (iSAT) where I mainly work with James Martin, Martha Palmer, and Boulder NLP Group . In iSAT, I am fortunate to have the opportunity to collaborate with and receive guidance from numerous brilliant researchers across various disciplines and institutions. Their diverse expertise has greatly enriched my experience and allowed me to broaden my horizons in this intellectual journey. Before this, I obtained my Ph.D. from the Kahlert School of Computing at the University of Utah, where I worked with Vivek Srikumar and the Uath NLP Group. My research interests centered around natural language processing and machine learning. I study methods for predicting structured representations of natural language text, e.g. Semantic Parsing, Dialogue System.
